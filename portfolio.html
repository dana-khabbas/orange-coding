About Me

My interest in programming started from a simple curiosity about how websites and applications actually work. As I began learning more, that curiosity quickly grew into a passion for building things myself. I enjoy the process of turning ideas into real digital products and solving problems through code. Studying Computer Information Systems at the University of Jordan has allowed me to explore this passion while developing skills in web and mobile development.

What drives me most is the opportunity to create solutions that are both useful and meaningful. I enjoy learning new technologies and constantly improving my skills, whether it is designing clean user interfaces, developing mobile applications using Flutter, or working with programming languages like Java. Looking ahead, my goal is to grow into a well-rounded developer who not only writes efficient and reliable code but also contributes positively to the teams and communities around me. I aim to build applications that provide real value to users while continuously learning and expanding my knowledge in modern technologies and software development practices.
